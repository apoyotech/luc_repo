## 1. What this add-on is

`luc_kodi` is a front-end (a user interface) for third-party services that
**you** configure with **your own accounts**. These services include:

- Debrid services (for example Real-Debrid, AllDebrid, Premiumize, TorBox)
- Torrent indexers and metadata providers

The add-on organises and displays information and connects to the services you
have set up. It is not a content provider.

## 2. The add-on hosts no content and includes no links

This add-on does **not** host, store, upload, stream or distribute any media
files. It does **not** ship, embed or include any links, magnets or addresses
pointing to specific content. Every source shown is located and resolved **at
run time** by the third-party services that you have configured with your own
credentials. Without your own accounts and configuration, the add-on returns no
playable sources.
