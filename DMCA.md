# DMCA & Copyright Policy

**Project:** `plugin.video.luc_kodi` / `luc_repo`
**Maintainer:** apoyotech

This project respects the intellectual property rights of others and asks its
users to do the same.

## This project hosts no content

Before submitting a notice, please note: this repository and this add-on **do
not host, store or distribute any media**, and they **do not contain any links,
magnets or addresses to specific content**. The add-on is a front-end that
connects to third-party services configured by each user with their own
accounts. There is therefore no hosted file or embedded link in this project
that can be removed.

Material returned during use originates entirely from third-party services that
are outside the control of this project. Notices about that material are best
directed to those services or to the relevant hosting providers.

## Reporting a concern

If you are a copyright owner (or authorised to act on behalf of one) and you
believe that something in **this repository** infringes your rights, you can
report it by opening an issue:

➡️ **https://github.com/apoyotech/luc_repo/issues**

To help us respond, please include:

1. Identification of the copyrighted work you believe is affected.
2. Identification of the specific material **in this repository** that you are
   referring to (file path, URL or commit).
3. Your contact information.
4. A statement that you have a good-faith belief that the use is not authorised
   by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law.
5. A statement that the information in your notice is accurate.

Every good-faith report will be reviewed and answered as promptly as is
reasonably possible, including removing or correcting any material in this
repository that is found to infringe.

## GitHub's process

This project is hosted on GitHub. GitHub operates its own DMCA takedown process,
which rights holders may use independently of this policy. See GitHub's
documentation for details.

## Counter-notices

If material is removed and you believe in good faith that it was removed in
error, you may respond through the same issue channel above, explaining why, so
that the matter can be reviewed.
